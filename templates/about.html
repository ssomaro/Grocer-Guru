About

Welcome to Grocer Guru, an intelligent shopping analyzer dedicated to enhancing your grocery shopping experience. At Grocer Guru, we believe in using data in making informed food choices to promote better health and wellbeing.

How It Works

  • Upload Your Receipt: Simply scan and upload your receipt from Kroger.
  • Analysis: Our system use vision Api to identify and list the items you've bought.
  • Nutritional Insights: We match each item with our comprehensive database of nutritional values.
  • Dashboard: View a personalized dashboard that showcases the nutritional makeup of your purchases.

Database

At the core of Grocer Guru is our nutritional information database, built from over 35 hours of dedicated scraping efforts. This robust database encompasses of more than 15,000 products available at Kroger, each cataloged with precise nutritional values. I plan to open source this database to help others build cool applications.
